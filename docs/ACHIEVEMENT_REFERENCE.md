# 🏆 Achievement Reference Guide

Your complete guide to unlocking all achievements in LifeGoalApp's gamification system.

## 📋 Complete Achievement List

### 🔥 Streak-Based Achievements

#### Week Warrior (Bronze) 🥉

**Requirement:** Maintain a 7-day streak

**Rewards:**
- 100 XP
- 50 Points
- +1 Streak Freeze 🛡️

**Description:** "Keep the fire burning for a full week"

**Tips:**
- Enable daily reminders in settings to never miss a day
- Include at least one easy habit in your daily routine
- This is your first major milestone - stay consistent!
- The streak freeze reward will protect your future progress

**Unlock Strategy:**
1. Complete at least 1 habit, check-in, or journal entry each day
2. Check your streak counter daily: 🔥 1 → 🔥 2 → ... → 🔥 7
3. On day 7, achievement unlocks automatically
4. Celebration notification appears with confetti! 🎉

---

#### Fortnight Fighter (Bronze) 🥉

**Requirement:** Maintain a 14-day streak

**Rewards:**
- 200 XP
- 100 Points

**Description:** "Two weeks of dedication!"

**Tips:**
- Use your Week Warrior streak freeze wisely
- Plan ahead for busy weekends
- Set morning alarms to complete habits early
- Track your progress daily to stay motivated

**Unlock Strategy:**
1. Continue your Week Warrior streak
2. Maintain consistency for another 7 days
3. If you slip up, use a streak freeze to protect your progress
4. Achievement unlocks on day 14

---

#### Consistency King (Gold) 🥇

**Requirement:** Maintain a 30-day streak

**Rewards:**
- 500 XP
- 250 Points
- +1 Streak Freeze 🛡️

**Description:** "A full month of excellence"

**Tips:**
- This is a gold-tier achievement - expect a challenge
- Build a freeze bank (3 freezes) before attempting
- Set up accountability partners (Phase 3 feature)
- Create habit stacking routines for reliability
- Celebrate weekly milestones to maintain motivation

**Unlock Strategy:**
1. Master the Fortnight Fighter streak
2. Maintain an additional 16 days of consistency
3. Use freezes strategically during expected busy periods
4. Achievement unlocks on day 30 with bonus rewards

**Why It Matters:**
- Proves you can build lasting habits
- 30 days is the scientifically backed habit formation period
- Opens the door to elite-tier achievements

---

#### Century Streak (Diamond) 💎

**Requirement:** Maintain a 100-day streak

**Rewards:**
- 1000 XP
- 500 Points

**Description:** "Elite dedication - 100 days strong!"

**Status:** **LEGENDARY** 🏆

**Tips:**
- This is the ultimate streak achievement
- Requires near-perfect discipline for over 3 months
- Build all possible streak freezes before attempting
- Create rock-solid daily routines
- Track your progress weekly to spot trends
- Join support communities (Phase 3)

**Unlock Strategy:**
1. Achieve Consistency King first
2. Maintain streak for an additional 70 days
3. Use analytics to identify your most vulnerable days
4. Plan freeze usage for known busy periods
5. Achievement unlocks on day 100

**Hall of Fame:**
Only the most dedicated users reach this milestone. Your name could be on the leaderboard! (Phase 2)

## 🗺️ Achievement Progression Path

### Recommended Path for New Users

#### Week 1: Foundation Building
```
Day 1:  Getting Started ✅ (Complete 1 habit)
        └─ Reward: 10 XP

Day 7:  Week Warrior ✅ (7-day streak)
        └─ Reward: 100 XP + Streak Freeze

Week 1: Habit Builder ✅ (10 habits)
        └─ Reward: 50 XP
```

**Week 1 Total:** ~160+ XP → **Level 2-3**

---

#### Week 2: Consistency Building
```
Day 14: Fortnight Fighter ✅ (14-day streak)
        └─ Reward: 200 XP

Week 2: Continue habit building
        └─ Progress toward Consistency Pro
```

**Week 2 Total:** ~200+ XP → **Level 3-4**

---

#### Month 1: Intermediate Milestones
```
Day 30: Consistency King 🥇 (30-day streak)
        └─ Reward: 500 XP + Streak Freeze

Month:  Visionary ✅ (1 goal achieved)
        └─ Reward: 50 XP

Month:  Consistency Pro 🥈 (50 habits)
        └─ Reward: 150 XP
```

**Month 1 Total:** ~900+ XP → **Level 5-7**

---

#### Month 3: Advanced Progress
```
Month 3: Century Club 🥈 (100 habits)
         └─ Reward: 300 XP

Goals:   Goal Crusher 🥇 (5 goals)
         └─ Reward: 400 XP
```

**Month 3 Total:** ~700+ XP → **Level 8-10+**

---

#### Month 4: Elite Status
```
Day 100: Century Streak 💎 (100-day streak)
         └─ Reward: 1000 XP

Status:  LEGENDARY 🏆
```

---

### Speedrun Path (Aggressive)

For competitive users aiming to unlock everything quickly:

**Week 1:**
- Complete 5 habits daily → Habit Builder unlocked
- Maintain perfect streak → Week Warrior unlocked
- **Total:** ~350 XP

**Week 2:**
- Complete 5 habits daily → Progress to Consistency Pro
- Perfect streak continues → Fortnight Fighter unlocked
- **Total:** ~550 XP

**Month 1:**
- Complete 3-5 habits daily → Century Club unlocked
- Perfect streak → Consistency King unlocked
- Complete 2-3 quick goals → Progress to Goal Crusher
- **Total:** ~1500 XP → **Level 10+**

**Month 3-4:**
- Perfect streak → Century Streak unlocked
- Complete final goals → Goal Crusher unlocked
- **Total:** ~3000+ XP → **Level 15+**

---

### Casual Path (Relaxed)

For users taking a steady, sustainable approach:

**Month 1-2:**
- Getting Started, Week Warrior, Habit Builder
- Focus on habit formation, not speed

**Month 3-6:**
- Fortnight Fighter, Consistency Pro
- Complete first goal (Visionary)
- Build streak freezes

**Month 6-12:**
- Consistency King, Century Club
- Work toward Goal Crusher
- Prepare for Century Streak attempt

**Year 1+:**
- Century Streak unlocked
- Goal Crusher achieved
- **Legendary status** 🏆

## 📜 Contract Achievements

Unlock these achievements through the Commitment Contract system. Contracts require staking gold or tokens on completing a habit or goal — these achievements reward those who honour their commitments.

---

### First Contract Kept (Bronze) 🥉

**ID:** `contract_first_kept`

**Requirement:** Successfully complete your first commitment contract

**Rewards:**
- 50 XP

**Description:** "A promise made, a promise kept."

**Tips:**
- Start with a Classic contract on an existing habit you already do daily
- Choose a modest stake (10–20 gold) for your first contract
- Keep the target count low (1× daily) to maximise your chance of success
- This achievement also unlocks the **Contract Scroll** 📜 in your Zen Garden

**Unlock Strategy:**
1. Open the Contracts tab in the Score section
2. Tap "New Contract" and choose Classic type
3. Select a habit you complete reliably
4. Set a small stake and a 7-day window
5. Complete the habit every day until the window closes
6. Achievement unlocks on successful evaluation

---

### 5 Contract Windows in a Row (Silver) 🥈

**ID:** `contract_5_streak`

**Requirement:** Successfully complete 5 consecutive contract evaluation windows

**Rewards:**
- 150 XP

**Description:** "Consistency is a superpower."

**Tips:**
- Windows can span multiple contracts — only consecutive successes count
- Use Grace Days (max 2) to protect against single off-days
- Redemption contracts can help recover from a miss without breaking the streak

**Unlock Strategy:**
1. Complete your first contract (First Contract Kept)
2. Immediately start a second contract on the same or new habit
3. Maintain success across 5 consecutive windows
4. Achievement unlocks after the 5th consecutive successful evaluation

---

### 10 Contract Windows Streak (Gold) 🥇

**ID:** `contract_10_streak`

**Requirement:** Successfully complete 10 consecutive contract evaluation windows

**Rewards:**
- 300 XP

**Description:** "A decade of windows, every one kept."

**Tips:**
- This requires sustained discipline across multiple contract cycles
- Consider switching to weekly cadence contracts for longer windows
- Build a streak freeze bank before attempting this milestone
- Escalation contracts can turn a close-call into motivation fuel

**Unlock Strategy:**
1. Achieve the 5-Window Streak first
2. Continue your momentum for another 5 consecutive successful windows
3. Achievement unlocks after the 10th consecutive success

---

### Perfect Contract Month (Gold) 🥇

**ID:** `contract_perfect_month`

**Requirement:** Complete every contract evaluation window in a calendar month without a miss

**Rewards:**
- 400 XP

**Description:** "Thirty days, zero misses. Extraordinary."

**Tips:**
- Start at the beginning of a month for a clean attempt
- Keep active contracts realistic — 1–2 with achievable targets
- Avoid Sacred contracts during your attempt (higher stakes = higher risk)
- Multi-Stage contracts count each stage completion toward the month

**Unlock Strategy:**
1. On the 1st of a month, ensure you have at least one active contract
2. Complete every single evaluation window that closes during the month
3. Achievement unlocks automatically when the month ends with no misses

---

### Sacred Contract Keeper (Diamond) 💎

**ID:** `contract_sacred_keeper`

**Requirement:** Successfully complete a Sacred contract (🔱)

**Rewards:**
- 1000 XP

**Description:** "You swore an oath to the universe. And you kept it."

**Status:** **LEGENDARY** 🏆

**Tips:**
- Sacred contracts carry 3× the stake in both reward and penalty — prepare carefully
- You can only create 2 Sacred contracts per calendar year — choose wisely
- Sacred contracts also unlock the **Sacred Oath Stone** 🔱 in your Zen Garden
- Only attempt a Sacred contract on a goal you are *absolutely certain* you can achieve
- Build a long streak of regular contract completions before attempting Sacred

**Unlock Strategy:**
1. Achieve First Contract Kept first
2. Build your Reliability rating to at least Dependable (50%+)
3. Tap "New Contract" and select the Sacred (🔱) type
4. Set a meaningful stake and confirm the 2-per-year ceremony
5. Complete every evaluation window for the contract's full duration
6. Achievement and Zen Garden item unlock on successful evaluation
