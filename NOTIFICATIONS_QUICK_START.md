### Step 1: Install the App (PWA)

**On iPhone (iOS 16.4+):**
1. Open Safari and go to your LifeGoal App
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
3. Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

**On Android:**
1. Open Chrome and go to your LifeGoal App

3. Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen"

### Step 2: Enable Notifications

1. **Open the app** from your home screen
2. **Go to "Set Up Habits"** tab
3. **Create a new habit** or edit an existing one
4. **Go to Step 3** (Targets & Reminders)
5. **Click "Enable Push Notifications"** button
6. **Allow notifications** when asked
7. **Set reminder times** (e.g., 08:00 for morning reminder)
8. **Save your habit**

### Step 3: Test It!

1. **Click "Test Notification"** in the habit wizard
2. **Check your phone** - you should see a notification
3. **Tap the notification** to open the app

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

**Not getting notifications?**
- Make sure you clicked "Enable Push Notifications"
- Check your phone's notification settings
- Ensure Do Not Disturb is off
- Verify reminder time is in the future

**Can't enable notifications?**
- Update your browser/OS to latest version
- Check if notifications are blocked in browser settings
- Try using Chrome, Firefox, or Safari

**Notifications late or not arriving?**
- This is normal for free hosting - notifications may be delayed
- For reliable delivery, the app needs to be deployed with proper CRON setup
