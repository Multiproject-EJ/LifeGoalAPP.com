The Year Wheel

Think Apple's activity rings, but for your year. Several calendars run at once as concentric rings — Work, Body, Creative — each carved into its own periods, all sharing one clock face. A single "today" hand sweeps across every ring. Hover a ring, flip the star tier, tap the badge to see the sequence.
